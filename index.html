Hidden Nordvest - Your local guide around the neighborhood

Nordvest is a vibrant, diverse district in Copenhagen, known for cozy cafés, experimental restaurants, lively bars, local yoga studios, relaxing saunas, and weekend second-hand markets (loppemarked). This mini-guide highlights events, places and local tips to help you explore the neighborhood I call home for over 2 years by now.

💌 A love letter to Nordvest’s best kept secrets.
✨ Hidden street corners, flavors and vibes.
🗓️ Always keeping you up-to-date on events and more.

I’ve been living in Nordvest for quiet some time now, and I couldn’t be happier. This place truly feels like home to me. From the friendly faces at local cafés to the ever-changing street art, every corner hides a new story. In May 2025 I decided to share more of its beauty and hidden corners with all of you, so you can experience the magic that makes Nordvest so special.
