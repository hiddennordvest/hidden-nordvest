About Nordvest

Nordvest is a vibrant, diverse district in Copenhagen — known for cozy cafés, experimental restaurants, lively bars, local yoga studios, relaxing saunas, and weekend second-hand markets (loppemarked). This mini-guide highlights events, places and local tips to help you explore the neighborhood.
